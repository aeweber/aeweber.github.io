Fly Fishing History and Techniques

British origins

First references to fishing with flies originated in England in the 13th century.

Japanese Tenkara

This is the traditional Japanese method of fly-fishing. Tenkara originated in the mountains of Japan as a way to harvest the local fish, Ayu, trout and char for selling and providing a meal to their guests.

Saltwater Fly Fishing

Done with heavier tackle than that which is used for freshwater fishing, both to handle the larger, more powerful fish, and to accommodate the casting of larger and heavier flies.

Today: greater access

Technical develpments, wider range of gear and new destinations have opened the doors of this sport to more people around the world.
